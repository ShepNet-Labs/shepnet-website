About

A founder led practice, built on plain advice.

ShepNet Security is a solo cybersecurity consulting practice based in Atlanta, Georgia. The work is hands on and direct. When you hire ShepNet Security, you work with the person doing the assessment, start to finish.

Why this practice exists

Small businesses get a raw deal on security. The advice aimed at them is usually written for large companies, padded with jargon, or tied to a product someone wants to sell. The result is owners who feel uneasy but have no clear sense of what to actually do.

I started ShepNet Security to fix that gap. The focus is narrow on purpose. One well defined assessment, delivered carefully, that gives a business owner an honest read on their risk and a plan they can act on. No pressure, no upsell, no products to push.

The goal is simple. You should finish knowing more about your own business and exactly what to do next.

How I work

Every engagement is consulting with a clear beginning and end. I assess how your business is set up, design practical improvements, document them in language you can use, and hand the work back to you so you stay in control.

I do not run monitoring, I do not operate your systems for you, and I do not promise that any single change makes a business immune to attack. What I offer is honest expertise and a plan you can trust, so the decisions stay yours.
